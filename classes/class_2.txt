Class Salad

Attributes:
dressing (string)
croutons (boolean)
salad_size (integer)
blue_cheese (boolean)


Methods:
change_dressing (changes the type of dressing)
decline.croutons (changes croutons from yes to no)
choose_size (allows you to select a different size of salad)
add.blue_cheese (changes blue_cheese value from no to yes)
